Mesh upper with honeycomb-shaped foam pads Lace-up design Fully collapsible upper Signature Swoosh logo on the sides Full-length midsole for durability Rubber outsole Item Number: 747108-600

The Nike Men's Juvenate feature a fully collapsible sports mesh upper which is ideal for packing into your gym bag or for when you need to pack light for a weekend away.

The no-tounge, sock like design offers supreme comfort while the full length midsole adds durability.
